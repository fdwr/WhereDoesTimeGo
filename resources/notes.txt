The icon making process:
- Download Visual Studio 2026 Image Library:
  - https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/visualstudio/designers/the-visual-studio-image-library?view=visualstudio
  - https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=35825
  - Extract: OpenFile.svg, Save.svg, SaveAs.svg, Edit.svg, RemoveCommand.svg...
- Convert to 32x32 via LunaSvgTest (https://github.com/fdwr/LunaSvgSampleTest). Copy to clipboard.
- Paste into Aseprite. Export to PNG.
- Convert to 32-BPP Bitmap using XnView 2021 (not IrfanView which doesn't don't preserve
  the alpha channel correctly), nor GIMP which causes LoadImage to fail. Exporting directly
  via LunaSvgTest to BMP also works, but the colors are slightly off because of premultiplied
  alpha issues. So XnView is the best option.
